Ndinya Calcifeeds

Calcium feeds are an integral part of ensuring the well-being and vitality of animals. Calcium, a vital mineral, plays a crucial role in numerous physiological processes within the body. By providing the necessary calcium feeds, we can support optimal bone formation and help prevent bone-related disorders. Furthermore, calcium is involved in nerve signal transmission and muscle contraction, facilitating coordination and proper muscle function. In poultry, calcium feeds are especially important for eggshell formation, resulting in sturdy and durable shells that reduce breakage risks. For dairy animals, calcium supports milk production, ensuring high-quality and abundant milk for their young. At Ndinya Feeds, we recognize the significance of supplying animals with top-quality calcium feeds, formulated to meet their nutritional requirements. By prioritizing calcium intake, we can enhance the overall health and productivity of animals, enabling them to thrive in their respective environments.
